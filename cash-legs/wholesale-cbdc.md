# Wholesale CBDC (token form)

Central bank money issued directly on DLT. For interbank settlement only.

## Live programmes

- **Project Helvetia** — SNB CHF on SDX, live since 2023, extended to 2027
- **Project Mariana** — SNB + BdF + MAS multi-CBDC FX (PoC done)
- **Project Jura** — SNB + BdF cross-border CHF/EUR (PoC done)
- **Project Agorá** — BIS + 7 CBs (incl ECB, BoE) — tokenized correspondent (in progress)
- **mBridge** — multi-currency wholesale CBDC for cross-border (HK, Thai, UAE, China, Saudi)

## Properties

- Central bank money — highest tier (no credit risk, no reserve mismatch)
- Distributed only to authorized banks
- Atomic DvP via tokenized securities (Helvetia model)
- Programmable money (smart contracts can trigger CB money flows)

## Bank ops

- Subscribe to wholesale CBDC tokens via SIC (debit reserve, credit DLT wallet)
- Use for settlement on SDX
- Redeem to SIC reserves on need
