# Retail CBDC

Central bank money for public. Distributed via banks/EMIs.

## Live + planned

- **Digital euro** — preparation phase since Nov 2023; ECB go-ahead 2026-2027 expected; earliest issuance 2027-2028
- **Digital pound** — UK consultation, no decision
- **e-CNY** — China at scale pilot
- **Sand Dollar / JAM-DEX / eNaira** — small-economy live

## Cash mgmt impact

- Banks become distributors (mandatory if EU credit institution)
- Holding cap (likely €3K) limits disintermediation
- New wallet product, new compliance integration
