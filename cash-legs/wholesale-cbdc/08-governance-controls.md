# 08 — Governance, controls, internal audit

Governance over wholesale CBDC participation aligns with how the bank governs other FMI participations (RTGS, CSDs, CCPs), with additional considerations for DLT and key-management novelty.

## 1. Board and committees

- Board awareness of wCBDC participation strategy.
- Risk Committee oversight of risk appetite, KRIs, incidents.
- Audit Committee oversight of audit coverage.
- Tech / Operational Resilience Committee for ICT, DORA, change governance.
- Treasury / ALCO oversight of intraday liquidity treatment.
- Reporting cadence: monthly executive committee; quarterly board committees.

## 2. Three lines of defence

- 1LoD: treasury, payments ops, tech own controls.
- 2LoD: risk + compliance independent oversight.
- 3LoD: internal audit cycle covering wCBDC.

## 3. Roles and responsibilities

- Treasury / Head of Liquidity for product ownership.
- CTO / Head of Engineering for integration.
- CISO for cyber and key custody.
- CRO for risk framework.
- CCO / MLRO for compliance overlay.
- General Counsel for FMI-rulebook and CB-agreement matters.
- Head of Operations for run-the-bank.

## 4. Policy framework

- wCBDC participation policy.
- FMI engagement policy.
- Key management and ceremony policy.
- Change management for DLT participations.
- Reviews at least annually.

## 5. Standards and frameworks

- CPMI-IOSCO PFMI (where the platform is a designated FMI).
- Basel Principles for Operational Resilience.
- DORA (EU) for ICT and third-party risk.
- ISO 27001, ISO 22301, ISO 31000.
- NIST CSF 2.0 / FFIEC where applicable.

## 6. Internal audit

- wCBDC participation in audit universe.
- Annual coverage of: integration controls, key ceremonies, reconciliation, incident response, FMI-rulebook compliance.
- Thematic reviews on DvP / PvP flows.

## 7. External assurance

- Statutory audit covers wCBDC sub-ledger and GL impact.
- ISAE 3402 / SOC reports from FMI / CB where provided.
- Penetration testing including TLPT.

## 8. Issue and incident management

- Centralised issue log.
- Incident playbooks coordinated with CB / FMI.
- Lessons-learned feeds policy and control redesign.

## 9. Training and culture

- Role-based training on DLT participation.
- New-joiner module for treasury, payments ops, tech.

## 10. Decision logs

- ADRs for participation decisions.
- Key ceremony minutes.
- Smart contract template adoption decisions.
