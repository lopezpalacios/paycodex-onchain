# ERC-7281 — xERC20 (Cross-chain Fungible)

Standard for ERC-20 tokens with native cross-chain semantics. Sovereign issuer controls bridges.

## Why

- USDC needed CCTP (Circle's bespoke); xERC20 standardizes for any issuer
- Multiple bridges supported with per-bridge limits
- Issuer retains control vs lock-and-mint trust assumption

## Cash mgmt use

- Tokenized deposit issued on multiple chains
- Cross-chain unified balance for treasury
- L1 + L2 + permissioned chain consistency
