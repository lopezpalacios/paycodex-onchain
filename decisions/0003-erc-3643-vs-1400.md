# ADR 0003 — Compliance standard: ERC-3643 vs ERC-1400

**Status**: Proposed
**Date**: 2026-04-25

## Decision

**ERC-3643 (T-REX) primary. ERC-1400 only for legacy compatibility.**

## Reasoning

- T-REX has on-chain identity (ONCHAINID) — better Travel Rule integration
- T-REX has composable compliance modules
- Tokeny dominant + bank-friendly vendor
- ERC-1400 older, less mature ecosystem
