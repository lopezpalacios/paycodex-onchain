# Basel III crypto exposure

BCBS final rules Dec 2022, in force 2025.

## Two groups

- **Group 1** — tokenized traditional assets (stablecoins meeting redemption rules) — standard RWA treatment
- **Group 2** — other (BTC, ETH, non-compliant stablecoins) — punitive 1250% RWA (basically dollar-for-dollar capital)

## Cap

- Group 2 exposures capped at 1% of Tier 1 capital

## Implications

- Banks must classify every token holding
- Tokenized deposit issuance: standard banking activity (not Group 2)
- USDC may qualify as Group 1 if reserve rules met
- Drives bank product design strongly toward Group 1
