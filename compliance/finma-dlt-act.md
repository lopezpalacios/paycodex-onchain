# FINMA DLT Act (Switzerland)

Federal Act on Adaptation of Federal Law to Developments in Distributed Ledger Technology. In force 2021.

## Key components

- **Ledger-based securities** — securities can exist natively on DLT
- **DLT trading facility license** — new license category for DLT exchanges (SDX is first)
- **Banking license bankruptcy treatment** — assets on DLT segregated in bank insolvency
- **AMLA application** — DLT trading platforms qualify as financial intermediaries

## Why CH leads

- DLT Act + FinTech license + Crypto Valley ecosystem
- SNB + SIX collaboration via Project Helvetia
- Clear legal status for ledger-based securities
- Bank ecosystem (Sygnum, SEBA — both regulated CH banks doing crypto natively)

## Cash mgmt impact

- Tokenized deposit (Sygnum DCHF) operates under clear framework
- Wholesale CBDC (Helvetia) leverages this
- Securities tokenization clear path
