# EU DLT Pilot Regime

EU 2022/858. Sandbox for DLT-based financial market infrastructure.

## Scope

- DLT MTF (Multilateral Trading Facility) on DLT
- DLT SS (Settlement System) on DLT
- DLT TSS (combined trading + settlement)

## Caveats

- Volume caps (per-instrument: €1bn equity / €2bn bonds; aggregate: €6bn issuer market cap)
- Permissioned chains only typically
- Existing reg dispensations available case-by-case

## Status

- In force 2023
- Approvals slow — 4-5 entities authorized as of 2025
- Industry pushing for caps to be raised
