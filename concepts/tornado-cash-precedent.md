# Tornado Cash — sanctions precedent

Aug 2022, OFAC sanctioned Tornado Cash smart contract addresses. First time a smart contract was placed on SDN list.

## Implication

- Smart contract addresses can be sanctioned
- Banks + VASPs must screen addresses, not just owners
- US courts later ruled against immutable contract sanctioning (5th Circuit, Nov 2024) — contract delisted, but precedent for individuals running it remains

## Cash mgmt impact

- Pre-tx address screening required (not just KYC)
- Can flag interactions with mixers / privacy pools as risk signal
- Bank refuses transfers to known sanctioned smart contracts
