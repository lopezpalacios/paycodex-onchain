# Proof of Reserves

On-chain attestation that stablecoin / fund / token is fully backed.

## Patterns

- **Aggregated PoR feed** — Chainlink push aggregate reserve total
- **Address transparency** — publish reserve wallet addresses, anyone can verify
- **ZK-PoR** — prove without revealing individual customer balances (Merkle proof per customer)

## Use

- Stablecoin issuer transparency
- Tokenized fund transparency (BUIDL has Chainlink PoR feed)
- Bank's tokenized deposit attestation (bank balance sheet matches issued tokens)
